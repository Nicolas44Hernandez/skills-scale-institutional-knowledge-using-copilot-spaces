# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
The Change Manager oversees all project change requests, ensures proper documentation, and facilitates communication between stakeholders regarding changes.

### Responsibilities
- Review and document all change requests
- Assess impact of proposed changes
- Communicate accepted changes to all impacted parties
- Maintain change log and version history

### Goals
- Maintain project stability through controlled change processes
- Minimize disruption from unplanned changes
- Ensure transparent communication about changes across all stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Change impact assessments and approval requests
- Change log updates and version history documentation
- Regular sync with Project Manager and Risk Owner on change implications

### Collaborates with
- Project Manager for planning and change impact assessment
- Engineering Lead for technical impact evaluation
- Risk Owner for risk evaluation when implementing changes

---

## Risk Owner

### Role Summary
The Risk Owner is accountable for continuous monitoring of assigned risks, maintaining risk registers, and updating mitigation plans.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor specific project risks continuously
- Develop and maintain mitigation strategies
- Report risk status during project reviews
- Escalate critical risks to appropriate stakeholders

### Goals
- Proactively identify and address potential project threats
- Minimize impact of realized risks on project outcomes
- Maintain comprehensive risk documentation and mitigation plans

### Typical Communication
- Risk status reports and escalations
- Risk register updates and mitigation plan documentation
- Regular risk reviews with QA Lead and Project Manager

### Collaborates with
- Project Manager for risk status reporting and mitigation planning
- QA Lead for regular risk reviews and quality impact assessment
- Stakeholders for critical risk escalations

---

## Communications Coordinator

### Role Summary
The Communications Coordinator manages all project-related communications, updates, and documentation to ensure efficient information flow between teams and external stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Ensure information flow is efficient between teams and external stakeholders
- Organize project documentation and announcements
- Manage communication channels and meeting notes
- Attend standups, reviews, and retrospectives to capture and distribute decisions

### Goals
- Maintain clear and consistent communication across all project stakeholders
- Ensure timely dissemination of project information and decisions
- Create comprehensive documentation of project activities and outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Meeting notes and decision documentation
- Project status updates and announcements
- Communication channel management and facilitation

### Collaborates with
- All core roles for status dissemination and information gathering

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
The QA Lead oversees all QA planning, execution, and defect triage, ensuring that delivery meets defined acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate testing activities across the project
- Ensure quality objectives are met
- Act as liaison between engineering and project management
- Manage defect tracking and resolution
- Ensure delivery meets defined acceptance criteria and standards

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality products that meet acceptance criteria
- Minimize defects in production releases
- Maintain efficient testing processes and clear quality metrics

### Typical Communication
- Test plans, results, and defect reports
- Quality status updates and testing timeline coordination
- Defect triage meetings and resolution tracking

### Collaborates with
- Project Manager for quality status and testing timeline coordination
- Engineering Lead for testing activities synchronization and defect resolution
- Risk Owner for regular risk reviews and quality impact assessment

---

## Role Interactions

This section defines how the new roles interact with existing roles to ensure clarity of responsibilities and effective collaboration.

### Change Management
- **Change Manager ↔ Project Manager**: Planning and change impact assessment
- **Change Manager ↔ Engineering Lead**: Technical impact evaluation
- **Change Manager ↔ Risk Owner**: Risk evaluation when implementing changes

### Risk Management
- **Risk Owner ↔ QA Lead**: Regular risk reviews and quality impact assessment
- **Risk Owner ↔ Project Manager**: Risk status reporting and mitigation planning

### Communications
- **Communications Coordinator ↔ All Roles**: Information gathering and dissemination

### Quality Assurance
- **QA Lead ↔ Project Manager**: Quality status and testing timeline coordination
- **QA Lead ↔ Engineering Lead**: Testing activities synchronization and defect resolution
